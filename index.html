Our Story

eldeeb’ was born out of a vision to provide a simple, yet warm and comfortable dining experience.

It all started with an idea of two childhood friends,football teammates and drinking lovers, to bring together each of their own strenghts, to create something they can share with others. With Chef ahmed – a natural artist, with an immense passion in culinary art, and ali – a CPA accountant with business acumen, eldeeb’ Coffee was born.
